About Us

Every café begins with a story. Ours started with a simple belief:
“Everyone deserves a great cup of coffee — without making their wallet cry.”
Back in August 2024, a 23-year old coffee lover decided to create a place where people could slow down, breathe, and enjoy life’s small joys — a cozy corner filled with the aroma of freshly brewed coffee and warm conversations.
THE BUYU BEANS — where coffee, comfort, and good vibes live together. ☕✨
